Tesla has signed a deal with Magnis Energy Technologies, an interesting new battery technology company based in Australia

The company is developing a graphite mine in Tanzania where it plans to get “ultra-high purity natural flake graphite” from the operation. Under the agreement, Tesla plans to purchase “a minimum of 17,500tpa starting from February 2025,” and the automaker has an option for “a maximum of 35,000tpa for a minimum 3-year term at a fixed price.”
